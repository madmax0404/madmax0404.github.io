NeurIPS 2024 - Lux AI Season 3 — LLM Agent (DeepSeek-R1-Distill-Qwen-1.5B)

I built a research-grade Lux AI Season 3 agent that replaces task-specific networks with a fine-tuned DeepSeek-R1-Distill-Qwen-1.5B language model trained through PPO self-play in the Hugging Face TRL stack. The notebooks walk from prompt engineering and environment analysis to custom trainer modifications, quantized deployment, and replay inspection, showing how reward shaping, action-vocabulary constraints, and iterative prompt tuning make an LLM viable in a 24x24 fog-of-war strategy game.
